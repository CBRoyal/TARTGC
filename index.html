How to Prepare for The Amazing Race's Challenges

Now when you're watching The Amazing Race's Challenges, you might think they'll be easy if you just pay attention and learn from the teams that are doing it. Well I can promise you this, they're not even if you have learn from your Amazing Race watching experience. But not to worry, this webpage will guide you on how to prepare for these exhausting, grueling, and emotional Amazing Race challenges so that way, you'll be able to maintain your position in the race.

Preparation for Physical Challenges

Now as you may know right now to this point, The Amazing Race involves a lot of physicality that includes running & scrambling to different locations, and the physical challenges that are presented on The Amazing Race. Here are some guidelines to help train your body for The Amazing Race:

  • Get into aerobic exercises like walking, running, swimming, balancing, climbing, and biking to increase your physicality and cardio endurance.

  • Run with your backpack with necessary stuff in it or a weighted vest to increase your endurance to really push your body forward.

  • Get into strength training exercises like lifting weights, squating, pull-ups, and press exercises to increase your strength endurance.

  • Include stretching exercises in your routine like yoga, plank poses, downward facing dog poses, and warrior poses to increase the flexibility of your body.

  • Run a rope course with your race teammate to not only increase your physicality, flexibility, and cardio endurance; but to also increase teamwork with your partner.


Dancing Challenges

Dance challenges on the race are one of the toughest types of challenges there is on The Amazing Race, especially if you don't have dancing experience. But not to worry as long as you watch and learn from the dance challenges from the show, and practice them yourself with great muscle memory, technique, and choreography; you'll be able to just glide through those dancing challenges in no time.

Preparation for Mental Challenges

Now The Amazing Race isn't just about the physicality, but it's also about the mentality as well. You need to be able to handle running the race under stress and pressure; have a good memory for memory challenges, and a strong attention to detail for detailed challenges. Here are some guidelines to help you train your mind for The Amazing Race:

  • Learn how to drive a manual car because in some countries, they only drive stick-shift vehicles. So, I would recommend you practice driving a stick-shift car to prepare yourself for any stick-shift self-driving parts on the show.

  • Try assembling furniture regularly so that way, you're prepared for any assembly challenges on the race. This not only increases your skills to build something unfamiliar, but to reinforce reading directions.

  • Reinforce your attention to detail by keeping your environment space tidy and organized, getting into artistic and drawing skills, replicating a certain picture properly with every single detail and aspect in it, reading a book, creating lists, or practicing mindfulness exercises.

  • Get into meditation exercises to increase your mentality to stay calm under stressful and uncomfortable events.

  • Get into memory exercises, puzzles, and/or trivia to help reinforce your brain for the race.

  • Learn as many lanuages as possible on language-learning apps like Duolingo & Babble

  • Learn how to read a map and navigate using only the map and not the GPS.

* An important thing to keep in mind: The Amazing Race might be a race to get every task done as fast as possible, but the most important tip is to fully read the whole clue and do exactly what is says, as it is the #1 rule of The Amazing Race so just take your time, read the whole clue properly to understand the challenge you're on properly, and get all these Race tasks done as efficiently as possible. Remember slow and steady wins the race.
